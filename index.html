Art Education



As a member of the artist collective Kunst Werk Stadt Berlin e.V. I offer workshops in painting, printmaking, and spraying for children, teenagers and adults. In partnership with Berlin.Mondiale and the evangelical parish of Gropiusstadt we run a silkscreen printing workshop in Gropiusstadt Berlin. I also give tours and practice art education in exhibitionary environments like art fairs and art festivals.
In my workshops I emphasize art as a tool to practice a differentiated reception in a group situation. I encourage workshop-participants to develop individual expressions rather then fulfill a certain canon. The goal is to experience self-efficacy.

I believe that the meaning of exhibitionary situations unfolds not only based on the background and identity of the artist, but of the mediator and viewer aswell. Meaning is connected to identity.
