Nora Brooke Roggausch




My work comprises drawings, paintings, and prints on paper and wood panels in various formats, aswell as murals, animations and installations. The work develops in layers. A mixture of oil paint, acrylic paint, spray paint, ink and color pencils - typically in vibrant and fluorescent colors - varies from translucent to opaque. Colored surfaces, bold brushstrokes and delicate markmaking are directly applied in a gestural and spontaneous flow. Abstract shapes of personal symbolism swirl around the paintground and create a sense of movement and speed, following their own gravity.


This is what my skin looks like from the inside.
